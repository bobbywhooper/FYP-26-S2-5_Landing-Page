Split, Reconstruct, & Share

Our web application enables users to securely split and reconstruct files using Shamir's Secret Sharing, a cryptographically proven algorithm that ensures no single fragment contains enough information to recover the original file. To further strengthen reliability, Reed-Solomon Error Correction is applied to each fragment , providing an additional layer of redundancy against data loss. Users can distribute their fragments to peers, who may then reconstruct the original file provided the minimum fragment threshold has been met.

Our Goals

  • Privacy

    Your files never exist as a whole on any single server. Every fragment is cryptographically meaningless alone, ensuring complete data privacy.

  • Reliability

    Lose a storage node, not your data. Reed-Solomon error correction ensures your files remain recoverable even when fragments are lost or unavailable.

  • Control

    You decide who holds your fragments and who can reconstruct your files. Access is only granted when the minimum threshold of fragments is met.
